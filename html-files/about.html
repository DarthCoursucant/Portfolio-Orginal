Amelia K Bujar, Born In 2002 And Hails From Koszalin, Poland Which Is Like Millions Of Other Small Towns In Europe With Nothing Really Happening. Lives In Bergen, Norway Since 2006

She Began Her Journey With Front-End Development In August, 2023 When She Began Taking Cources In Front-End Development At Noroff Bergen

She Has Very Nerdy Interests Such As Being A Star Wars Nerd, Reading Books, Playing Video Games, Watching Too Many Movies And Tv Shows, Listening Way Too Much Music And Of Course Codeing

During The Years She Done A Fair Shair Of Things Such As Joining Young Liberals Of Norway Which Is The Youth Political Organization That Is Under Norwegian Political Party Venstre. In August 2021 She Began A Degree In 3D & Anmation At Noroff Oslo. Before In Mai, 2022 Dropping Out. In Purpose To Take A Gap Year To Find Out What She Wanted To Do In Life.

During The Gap Year She Began Seeing A Lot Of Advertisements Of Codecademy On Social Media. That's When She Finally Decided To Become A Member Of Codecademy To Try To Learn Some Codeing Skills

The Codeing She Done At Codecademy In The Beginning Was A Way To Spend Too Much Free Time She Had During The Gap Year. But This Quickly Turned Into What She Wanted For Her Carrer. So She Applied For The Front-End Development Cource At Noroff Bergen And She Was Accepted Into The Cource And That's How Her Carrer Began In Front-End Development

In August, 2024 She Will Begin Her Last Year At Noroff, Bergen Studying Front-End Development Before Going Into The Front-End Fild Full-Time

In June 2024, She Began Taking Small Freelancing Jobs Where She Created Websites For Small Companies And Local Stores Which Didn't Have A Website. She Began Taking Freelancing Jobs To Gather Work Experience When She Still Was A Student In Purpose Of Making The Process Of Finding A Front-End Job Easier
